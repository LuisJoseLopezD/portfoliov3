About Me

When I was 14 years old I created my first website and during the process i received 2000 visits a day. That's when it all started. I always liked design and languages, so i ended up deciding to study the first, i abandoned it almost immediately, totally disappointed by how many people destroy creativity instead of encouraging it.

Then, challenging myself, i opted for Engineering, facing contents that i thought i would never be able to master. However, i managed to get out of all the drawbacks successfully, years later i opened my eyes, i had been in an obsolete educational system with teachers that didn't fit for the modern world, where they even denied the only constant in our lives which is change and where also the mistakes of past generations kept repeating themselves.

Therefore i came to the conclusion that the true education is the one you give yourself and the one that never ends because it is the one that really matters, for this reason i decided to keep myself in constant training and adaptation to any difficulty.
